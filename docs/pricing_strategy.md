# Pricing Strategy - AutoAsset AI

## Individual Products
Products are priced between **$9 and $49** based on complexity and value provided.

| Price Point | Product Types | Examples |
|-------------|---------------|----------|
| $9 | Simple checklists, basic templates | Emergency Contacts, Tenant Screening Checklist |
| $14 - $19 | Standard legal forms, maintenance logs | Move-in Checklist, Home Maintenance Log |
| $29 - $49 | Complex legal agreements, analysis tools | Standard Lease Agreement, Deal Analyzer Pro |

## Bundles
Bundles offer a curated selection of products at a discount (typically 20-35% off).

- **Landlord Starter Kit ($79)**: Essential forms for new landlords (Lease, Checklist, Screening Guide, Application).
- **Property Manager Pro Bundle ($149)**: Full set of landlord templates plus the Portfolio Tracker for managing multiple properties.
- **Homeowner Essentials ($49)**: Maintenance Log and Emergency Contacts — perfect for first-time homeowners.

## Subscriptions
The "Pro Membership" provides the best value for power users.

- **Monthly Subscription ($19/mo)**: Recurring access to all products.
- **Annual Subscription ($149/yr)**: All benefits of the monthly plan with a ~35% discount (effectively $12.40/mo).
