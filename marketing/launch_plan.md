# Steadfast Property Care - One-Page Launch Plan

## 1. Local SEO & Search Presence
- **Google Business Profile:**
    - Verify Redding, CA address and phone number.
    - Upload brand logo and hero imagery.
    - Add service categories: HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, Handyman.
- **Directory Listings:**
    - Create and sync profiles on **Yelp**, **Angi (formerly Angies List)**, and **Nextdoor** for the Redding area.
    - Ensure NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across all platforms.
- **On-Page SEO:** Target "Property maintenance in Redding" and "Emergency repair Redding" in meta titles.

## 2. Launch Week Social Media (3 Posts)
- **Post 1 (The Announcement):**
    - *Visual:* `logo.png` or team photo.
    - *Caption:* "We are officially LIVE! Steadfast Property Care is now serving Redding, CA and surrounding areas in Shasta County. Reliable, professional maintenance is just a click away. Check out our new site at [URL]!"
- **Post 2 (Emergency Focus):**
    - *Visual:* `social_post_emergency.png`.
    - *Caption:* "Plumbing leak at 2 AM in Redding? HVAC out on a holiday? We've got you covered. Our 24/7 emergency response team is ready when you need us most. Save our number: (555) 123-4567."
- **Post 3 (Landlord Focus):**
    - *Visual:* `landlord_services.png` or `social_post_plans.png`.
    - *Caption:* "Tired of managing a dozen different contractors in Shasta County? Landlords and property managers—our subscription plans provide regular inspections and priority service so you can focus on your business. Explore our Gold Plan today!"

## 3. Online Ad Strategy
- **Google Local Services Ads:**
    - Focus on high-intent keywords: "emergency plumber near me", "handyman for hire", "AC repair Redding".
    - Set a daily budget of $20-50 to start.
- **Facebook Retargeting:**
    - Target users in Redding/Anderson/Shasta Lake interested in "Real Estate Investing", "Property Management", and local "Homeowner" groups.
    - Use the "Maintenance Plans" graphic to drive traffic to the subscription page.

## 4. Operational Readiness
- **Lead Capture:** Ensure `server.js` is running and correctly logging leads to `team-db`.
- **Response Protocol:** Dispatch to follow up on all website leads within 2 business hours.
- **Feedback Loop:** Request a Google Review from the first 10 customers in Redding to establish early social proof.
