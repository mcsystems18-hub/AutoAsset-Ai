## Blog/FAQ Article: "The Essential Landlord Maintenance Checklist"
**Heading:** 5 Maintenance Tasks Every Landlord Should Complete Quarterly
**Content:**
Regular maintenance isn't just about keeping tenants happy—it's about protecting your investment. Small issues left unchecked can spiral into costly repairs that eat into your margins. Here are five essential tasks every landlord should perform every three months:

1. **HVAC Filter Replacement:** Dirty filters force units to work harder, leading to early failure and higher utility bills for your tenants.
2. **Leak Inspection:** Check under sinks, around toilets, and near water heaters. Catching a slow drip today prevents mold and floor damage tomorrow.
3. **Smoke & Carbon Monoxide Detectors:** Test every unit to ensure tenant safety and maintain legal compliance.
4. **Gutter Cleaning:** Prevent water from backing up under your roof or pooling near the foundation.
5. **Pest Entry Points:** Seal small cracks and crevices before they become a highway for unwanted visitors.
