# Steadfast Property Care - Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Essence
**Mission:** To provide property owners with a single, reliable point of contact for high-quality repairs and maintenance, ensuring peace of mind through transparency and professional expertise.
**Values:** Reliability, Transparency, Professionalism, Quality.

## 2. Visual Identity

### Logo
- **Primary Logo:** A minimalist house shape integrated with a shield/gear.
- **Clear Space:** Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the 'S' in 'Steadfast'.
- **Minimum Size:** 100px width for digital, 1 inch for print.

### Color Palette
- **Primary Navy:** `#1E293B` (Slate 800) - Represents stability and professionalism.
- **Action Blue:** `#2563EB` (Blue 600) - Used for call-to-actions and key highlights.
- **Background White:** `#F8FAFC` (Slate 50) - Keeps the look clean and modern.
- **Accent Charcoal:** `#334155` (Slate 700) - Used for secondary text and borders.

### Typography
- **Primary Typeface:** Inter or similar Sans-Serif (e.g., system-ui).
- **Headings:** Bold weights (700-800) for strong hierarchy.
- **Body:** Regular weight (400) for readability.

## 3. Brand Voice
- **Professional but Approachable:** We are experts, but we talk like neighbors.
- **Direct and Clear:** No jargon. We explain what needs to be fixed and why.
- **Reassuring:** We emphasize that we "handle it" so the client doesn't have to worry.

## 4. Imagery Style
- Clean, well-lit environments.
- Friendly, professional technicians in uniform.
- Focus on partnership (e.g., handshakes with clients) and precision work.
